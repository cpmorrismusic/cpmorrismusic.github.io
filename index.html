Testimonials   ⇓


"Dr. Morris is an inspirational mentor to young musicians. He helps them see possibilities that they may not know exist. He guides his students as they pursue their dreams. He encourages them to take risks in their musicianship, because he believes in his students and knows that, if they put their minds to it, they can accomplish whatever they set out to do. And he is there to help, as needed, every step along the way."

Paul Rayius, Music Educator, State College, Pennsylvania



"Christopher Morris is a man of artistic talents, he has skill, intelligence and imagination."

Phyllis Curtin, International Soprano Soloist, Former Dean, Boston University School for Fine Arts



"My own experiences with his music have left me feeling fulfilled as a performer. He has a gift for lyrical writing and the craft to exploit it."

Fredric Cohen, International Oboist, Professor, University of Massachusetts Amherst



"I find his compositions fresh and rewarding, both to hear and to perform. I have commissioned him twice for Boston University concerts."

Sam Pilafian, International Solo Tubist



"He is an excellent musician, very versatile, and has always maintained high quality of performance."

Roger Voisin, Former Principal Trumpet, Boston Symphony Orchestra
